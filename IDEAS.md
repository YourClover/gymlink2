# Gymlink Feature Ideas

## Training Intelligence

### AI Workout Suggestions

Analyze training history to suggest exercises for lagging muscle groups, recommend deload weeks, or auto-generate balanced programs.

### Superset / Circuit Mode

Group exercises into supersets, trisets, or circuits during workout execution with linked rest timers.

### Progressive Overload Tracker

Automatically suggest weight/rep increases based on recent performance trends (e.g., "You hit 3x8 at 80kg last 3 sessions — try 82.5kg").

### 1RM Calculator & Strength Standards

Estimate one-rep max from logged sets and show where the user stands relative to bodyweight-based strength standards (beginner/intermediate/advanced/elite).

## Fun & Gamification

### Gym Wrapped / Year in Review

Spotify Wrapped-style annual (or monthly) summary with fun stats: total tonnage, favorite exercise, most trained muscle, longest streak, etc.

### Workout Bingo

Weekly/monthly bingo card with challenges like "Train legs twice", "Hit a PR", "Try a new exercise", "Log RPE 9+".

### XP & Leveling System

Earn XP for workouts, consistency, volume milestones; level up with titles (Novice → Warrior → Titan → Legend).

### Badge Collections

Themed badge sets beyond achievements (e.g., "The Iron Collection" for hitting PRs on all big compounds, "Consistency King" for 30/60/90/365-day streaks).

## Social & Community

### Workout Reactions / Comments

Let followers react to or comment on completed workouts in the activity feed.

### 1v1 Challenges

Challenge a friend to a head-to-head duel (most volume in a week, first to hit X sets, etc.) with live progress tracking.

### Workout Templates Marketplace

Users can publish and rate workout plans; browse popular community programs.

## Body & Recovery

### Body Measurements Tracker

Track weight, body fat %, and circumference measurements over time with progress charts.

### Fatigue / Recovery Score

Based on recent volume, frequency, and RPE data, show a recovery score per muscle group ("Chest: Fully Recovered", "Legs: Still Fatigued").

### Rest Day Suggestions

Intelligently suggest rest days based on accumulated fatigue and training frequency.

## Quality of Life

### Workout Notes / Journal

A dedicated training journal for session reflections, form cues, or how you felt beyond just the mood rating.

### Exercise Substitution Suggestions

"Can't use the bench? Here are alternatives that hit the same muscles with available equipment."

### Plate Calculator

Enter a target weight, see which plates to load on each side of the bar.

### Timer Modes

AMRAP timer, EMOM timer, Tabata timer built into the workout screen.
